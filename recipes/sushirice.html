Sushi Rice

Learn how to make perfect sush rice with this simple step-by-step recipe.

Make a batch and craft your own sushi selection platter for entertaining.

  • Prep: 15 minutes
  • Cook: 15 minutes plus soaking.
  • Difficulty: Easy
  • Serves: 4
  • Gluten-free
  • Low fat
  • Vegan

Nutrition: Per serving

  • kcal 432
  • fat 1g
  • saturates 0g
  • carbs 99g
  • sugars 26g
  • fibre 3g
  • protein 6g
  • salt 3,7g (low)

Ingredients

For the sushi-su

  • 120ml rice vinegar or brown rice vinegar
  • 3 tbsp sugar seasoning, to taste (optional)

    For the rice

  • 450 Japanese rice (3 Japanese cups

Method

  1. Microwave potatoes on high for 10 minutes until tender.
  2. Leave to cool for 5 mins.
  3. Scoop the flesh in a bowl.
  4. Season with olive oil, lemon zest and juice to taste.
  5. Mix in the egg, salmon and parsley.
  6. Shape into small rounds 3cm wide and 1cm deep.
  7. Chill for 15 minutes.
  8. Dust each cake with the peppered flour.
  9. Fry over a low heat in a little oil for 2-3 mins on each side.
  10. Drain on kitchen paper.
  11. Serve garnished with salmon and parsley.
